Project: Beach girl

Creating a Bucket hat from used materials & scraps

In this project I was brainstorming of ways how to reuse old scraps from sewing projects, what to do with old clothes I'm not wearing and how to transform them. The remade piece had to be a piece which I could go out to a festival or some other fun event.

In this project I created sketches and played around with the materials until a friend gave me simple pattern of hers for a bucket hat. I cut out different color fabric pieces and sewed the patches first for each pattern piece, then I sewed the hat together. Materials I used were scraps from previous projects and some old pants.
