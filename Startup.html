Project: Startup

Creating a start-up, Branding, Web Design

Part 1: First part of the project was focused on creating an idea and developing a startup plan. The Startup plan included BMC, creating a prototype and developing our product. It was not easy to come up with an idea during three days. Our idea of the product was a water pedal with motorsounds to attract the dish washing experince by making it fun. Shortly after me and another member of the group understood that the car sounds dont make the dishwashing fun, but we decided on continuing with the idea, because of the project deadline.

Part 2: In the part 2. our project goal was to brand our idea on the market. The project included creating Content strategy and create SoMe posts, research consumer trends and come up with strong brand identity. In this project there were not specific roles and all fo tried to grab on everything. In this project I was focusing on target group research, creating personas and colors of the brand. In this project each of us coded a page.
