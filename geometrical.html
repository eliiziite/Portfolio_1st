Project: Geometrical Chick

Recreating a ballon arm blouse from a man's shirt

In this project I had an idea to recreate something new and stylish from a man's shirt I thrifted couple years ago. The Man's shirt was laying in my wardrobe for quite some time and I was bored of the oversized shirt itself. I wanted to bring the colorful geometric line pattern and silky fabric a new life.

I did the sketches and designed the shirt. My inspiration for the tailoring part was from Fashion Wizardy youtube channel.
